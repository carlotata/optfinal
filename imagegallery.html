FAVORITE ANIME SERIES

THAT TIME I GOT REINCARNATED AS A SLIME.

Satoru Mikami, a corporate worker, gets reincarnated as a slime with unique powers. He befriends a powerful dragon and leads beings in the Great Forest, forming the nation of Tempest.

ONE-PUNCH MAN

Saitama, a hero who defeats any enemy with a single punch, joins the Hero Association. Facing monsters and a threat from an alien invasion, Saitama battles to protect the world.

DRAGON-BALL

Follow the adventures of Goku as he searches for the Dragon Balls, battles strong opponents, and confronts powerful foes to protect Earth from various threats.

OVERLORD

In the virtual game world of YGGDRASIL, Momonga becomes trapped as his skeletal avatar. He explores a new world, Nazarick, seeking answers and dealing with the consequences of his near-limitless power.

KONOSUBA

Kazuma, reincarnated in a parallel world with a goddess, forms an unconventional party to face challenges. Their humorous adventures involve battling monsters and thwarting the Devil King.

ONE-PIECE

Follow the journey of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew as they search for the legendary treasure, One Piece. Encounter diverse characters, face powerful enemies, and explore a vast, adventurous world.
